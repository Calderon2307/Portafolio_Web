Cayman GT4

The Porsche Cayman GT4 is a sports car with mid-engine, rear-wheel drive, and a powerful 4.0-liter engine with 414 horsepower. It accelerates quickly and offers manual or automatic transmission options. Its design is aerodynamic and its interior focuses on the driving experience.
